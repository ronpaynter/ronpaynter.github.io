Broccoli Casserole

Contributed by Mrs. Norwood
INGREDIENTS:

1 pkg frozen brocolli
1/2 can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup mayo
1/2 cup grated cheese (parmesian)
1 egg
2 Tbsp chopped onion
Pepper to taste
Cheese Nips

DIRECTIONS:
  1. Cook brocolli.
  2. Add other ingredients.
  3. Place in casserole dish.
  4. Sprinke with crushed Cheese Nips.
  5. Cook for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
