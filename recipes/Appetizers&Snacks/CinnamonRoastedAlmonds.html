Cinnamon Roasted Almonds

Contributed by Ron Paynter
INGREDIENTS

1 egg white
1 teaspoon vanilla flavoring
3 cups whole almonds
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

DIRECTIONS
  1. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
  2. Lightly grease 2 cookie sheets.
  3. Lightly beat the egg white in a large bowl.
  4. Add vanilla flavoring, and beat until frothy but not stiff.
  5. Stir in nuts until well coated.
  6. Mix white sugar, brown sugar, salt, and cinnamon in a small bowl.
  7. Sprinkle sugar mixture over nuts; toss to coat. Spread evenly onto the prepared cookie sheets.
  8. Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes in the preheated oven, stirring occasionally, until golden.
  9. Allow to cool, then store nuts in an airtight container.
