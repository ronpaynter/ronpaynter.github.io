Pretzels

Contributed by June Paynter
INGREDIENTS

1 package yeast (2 1/4 tsp)
1 1/2 cups warm water
1 tsp salt
1 Tbsp sugar
4 cups flour
1 egg
Coarse salt

DIRECTIONS
  1. Dissolve yeast into warm water.
  2. Add salt, sugar.
  3. Blend in flour.
  4. Knead dough until smooth.
  5. Cut into small pieces.
  6. Roll into ropes, and twist into desired shape.
  7. Place on lightly greased cookie sheets.
  8. Brush pretzels with beaten egg.
  9. Sprinkle with coarse salt.
  10. Bake immediately at 425 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.
  11. Will make about 20 large pretzels.
