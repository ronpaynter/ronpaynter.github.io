Pizza Dough

Contributed by June Paynter
INGREDIENTS:

1 tsp dry yeast
1 1/4 cup warm water
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt (garlic, onion, or regular)
3 - 3 1/4 cups flour

DIRECTIONS:
  1. Add all ingredients in order, mix well (may need to add extra water).
  2. Knead, place in bowl that you've sprayed (Pam).
  3. Cover, let set for approx 1 hour - I've even used it after 10 minutes, but better if it sits longer.
  4. Makes 2 14x10 rectangles (400 degrees for 20 minutes).
