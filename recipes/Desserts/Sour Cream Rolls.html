Sour Cream Rolls

Contributed by June Paynter
INGREDIENTS:

1 cup warm water
1 pkg dry yeast
3/4 cup lukewarm sour cream (use half sour cream and half powdered milk mixture)
3 Tbsp sugar
1/3 tsp soda
1 tsp salt
1 large egg
3 Tbsp soft shortening (margarine)
3 cups flour
Brown sugar
Cinnamon
3 sticks margarine, melted.
2 tsp vanilla
2 dashes salt
1 (1 lb) box powdered sugar
Milk

DIRECTIONS:
  1. Combine sour cream and powdered milk mixture and set on low heat.
  2. Dissolve yeast in water, combine with sour cream mixture.
  3. Stir in remaining ingredients.
  4. Turn dough onto lightly floured surface and roll into an oblong, 24 inches x 6 inches.
  5. Spread with soft margarine, and sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  6. Roll up and slice.
  7. Place on cookie sheet, cover, and let rise for one hour.
  8. Bake at 375 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.
  9. While warm, frost with icing.
  10. To make icing, melt 1 stick margarine.
  11. Mix with 2 tsp vanilla, 2 dashes salt, and mix thoroughly.
  12. Add 1 box (1 lb) powdered sugar, and milk for an easy spreading consistency.
  13. Spread while warm.
  14. The roll recipe can be used to make Swedish Tea Rings or any of your favorite swee roll recipes.
