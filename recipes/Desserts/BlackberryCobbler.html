Blackberry Cobbler

Contributed by Donna Paynter
INGREDIENTS

! cup self-rising flour
2 cups sugar
1 cup milk
1 stick butter
4 cups blackberries or other fruit

DIRECTIONS
  1. Place butter in a 9x9 pan in a 350 degree F oven.
  2. While butter is melting, mix flour, sugar, and milk.
  3. Batter will be lumpy.
  4. Pour batter into pan with butter.
  5. Top with fruit.
  6. Cook until brown and sides pull away - about 45 minutes.
  7. Great with vanilla ice cream.
