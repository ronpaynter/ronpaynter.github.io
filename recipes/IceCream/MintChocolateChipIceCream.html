Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream

Contributed by Ron Paynter
INGREDIENTS:

1 quart half and half
2 14 oz cans sweetend condensed milk
1 Tbsp plus 1 tsp vanilla extract
1 16 oz package of semi-sweet chocolate mini-morsels, blended
15 pieces Andes mints, blended
Green food coloring

DIRECTIONS:
  1. Combind first five ingredients, mixing well.
  2. Add food coloring until desired color is achieved.
  3. Pour ice cream mixture into freezer can of 1 gallon hand-turned or electric freezer.
  4. Freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.
  5. Yield: 2 1/2 quarts.
