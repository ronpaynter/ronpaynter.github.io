Sugar Cookies

Contributed by June Paynter
INGREDIENTS:

2 cups brown or white sugar
1 heaping cup Crisco shortening
3 eggs
1 cup sour milk
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
5 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp vanilla

DIRECTIONS:
  1. Mix all ingredients.
  2. Bake at 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes.
