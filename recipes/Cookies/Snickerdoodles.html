Snickerdoodles

Contributed by Ron Paynter
INGREDIENTS

1 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp ground cinnamon
1 cup shortening
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp cream of tartar

DIRECTIONS
  1. In a shallow bowl, combine 1 Tbsp sugar and cinnamon, set aside.
  2. Cream shortening, gradually add 1 1/2 cups sugar, beating well.
  3. Add eggs, beat well.
  4. Stir in vanilla.
  5. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, soda, salt and cream of tartar.
  6. Add sifted ingredients to creamed mixture, stir until well blended.
  7. Shape dough into 1-inch balls and roll in cinnamon-sugar mixture.
  8. Place snickerdoodles 2 inches apart on lightly greased baking sheets.
  9. Bake snickerdoodles at 400 degrees F for 6 minutes or until lightly browned.
  10. Remove to wire racks to cool.
  11. Makes about 4 dozen snickerdoodles.
