Uncle Bob's French Toast

Contributed by Bob Maddy
INGREDIENTS

1 cup all purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup milk
2 eggs
8 slices bread

DIRECTIONS
  1. Mix together all the ingredients except the bread.
  2. Dip the bread and fry in 1/2 inch of cooking oil in a large frying pan until light brown on both sides.
  3. Remove, drain, and put in baking pan until all are cooked.
  4. May then be reheated in a hot oven just before serving.
