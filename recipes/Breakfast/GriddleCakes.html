Griddle Cakes

Contributed by June Paynter
INGREDIENTS

1 1/4 cups sifted flour
2 1/2 tsp baking powder
3 Tbsp sugar
3/4 tsp salt
1 egg, beaten (I omit)
1 cup milk
3 Tbsp vegetable oil

DIRECTIONS
  1. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, salt together.
  2. Combine egg and milk, add to dry ingredients, beat until smooth.
  3. Add oil, and mix.
  4. Pour cakes onto hot griddle, turn when full of bubbles.
