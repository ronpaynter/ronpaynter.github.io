Molasses Cookies

Contributed by June Paynter
INGREDIENTS

3/4 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1/3 cup molasses
2 1/3 cups flour
2 tsp soda
1 tsp (use less) ginger, cinnamon, cloves
1/4 tsp salt

DIRECTIONS
  1. Mix shortening, sugar, egg, and molasses.
  2. Sift flour, soda, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and salt.
  3. Mix well and roll into 1 inch balls.
  4. Dip tops in sugar and bake on ungreased baking sheet in 375 degree oven, 12 to 15 minutes.
