Chicken Enchiladas

Contributed by Donna Paynter
INGREDIENTS:

10-12 large flour tortillas
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 small can green chilis or green pepper
1 1/2 cups shredded cheese
2 cups cooked, shredded chicken

DIRECTIONS:
  1. Mix all ingredients.
  2. Place some in each tortilla, rolling it up.
  3. If any leftover, place down the center third.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes.
