Mud Pie

Contributed by Debbie Leathers
INGREDIENTS

24 Oreo cookies
1/4 cup plus margarine
3 squares chocolate
1/4 cup margarine
2/3 cup sugar
2/3 cup evaporated milk
1 tsp vanilla
pinch of salt
2 pints pralines 'n cream
3/4 cup toasted almonds

DIRECTIONS
  1. Crush whole cookies and add 1/4 cup plus margarine, blend well.
  2. Spread in 10 inch pie pan, chill for 2 hours.
  3. Melt chocolate and 1/4 cup margarine in double boiler.
  4. Stir in sugar, salt, and milk.
  5. Cook 4 minutes.
  6. Add flavoring, chill.
  7. Spread ice cream on crust.
  8. Pour chilled sauce over ice cream.
  9. Top with nuts, if desired.
  10. Chill overnight.
