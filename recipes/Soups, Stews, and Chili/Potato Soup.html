Potato Soup

INGREDIENTS:

1 Medium Onion, Chopped
2 Stalks Celery, Chopped
4 Tablespoons Butter
4 Medium Potatoes, Diced
1 Cup Water
2 Teaspoons Salt
1/4 Cup Flour
1/2 Teaspoon Pepper
1 Cup Sour Cream
2 1/2 Cups Milk

DIRECTIONS:
  1. On medium heat, cook onion and celery in butter.
  2. Add potatoes, water, and salt. Cover and cook 30 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
  3. Blend flour, pepper, and sour cream until smooth. Stir into potatoes. Add milk.
  4. Heat until boiling, stirring occasionally. Season to taste.
  5. Top with freshly crumbled bacon bits and shredded cheese, if desired.
