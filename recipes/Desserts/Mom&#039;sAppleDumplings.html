Mom's Apple Dumplings

Contributed by June Paynter
INGREDIENTS

Pastry for 9-inch two-crust pie
6 medium tart juicy apples
1 cup sugar
2 cups water
3 Tbsp butter
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 Tbsp butter

DIRECTIONS
  1. Heat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Cut pastry into 6 equal pieces, and roll out one at a time.
  3. Place each apple that has been pealed and cored onto each pastry section.
  4. Fill cavities of apples with mixture of sugar and cinnamon.
  5. Dot with butter.
  6. Boil the first four ingredients listed after apples together for three minutes.
  7. Bring opposite points of pastry up over the apple, overlap, moisten and seal.
  8. Place a little apart in baking dish.
  9. Pour hot syrup AROUND dumplings.
  10. Bake immediately 40 to 45 minutes, or until crust is nicely browned.
