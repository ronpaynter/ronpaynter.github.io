Peanut Blossoms

Contributed by June Paynter
INGREDIENTS

1 3/4 cups flour
1 tsp soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 egg
2 Tbsp milk
1 tsp vanilla
48 Hershey kisses

DIRECTIONS
  1. Combine all ingredients except candy in large mixing bowl.
  2. Mix on lowest speed of mixer until dough forms.
  3. Shape dough into balls, using a rounded tsp for each.
  4. Roll balls in sugar, place on ungreased cookie sheet.
  5. Bake at 375 for 10 to 12 minutes.
  6. Top each cookie immediately with a candy kiss.
