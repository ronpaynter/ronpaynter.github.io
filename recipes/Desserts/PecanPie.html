Pecan Pie

Contributed by Donna Paynter
INGREDIENTS

1 cup corn syrup
1 Tbsp butter
1/8 tsp salt
3 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 9-inch pie shell
1 cup chopped pecans

DIRECTIONS
  1. Put all above (except for pie shell and nuts) together and mix will with rotary beater.
  2. Add chopped nuts and stir well.
  3. Put in uncooked pie shell and cook at 325 degrees for about 1 hour.
