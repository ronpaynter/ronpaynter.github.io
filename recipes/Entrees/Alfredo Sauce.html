Alfredo Sauce

Contributed by Donna Paynter
INGREDIENTS:

1/2 cup milk
3/4 cup parmesan cheese
1/2 margarine
8 oz package cream cheese (lite)
Noodle of choice (great with Ziti)

DIRECTIONS:
  1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan.
  2. Heat on medium, mixing well, until all ingredients are blended.
  3. Pour over cooked noodles of choice.
